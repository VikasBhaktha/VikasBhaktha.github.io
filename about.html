About Me

I am a PhD Candidate in Civil Engineering at Wayne State University, with an expected graduation date of December 2026.

Professionally, I work as a Bridge Engineer at Jacobs Engineering Group, where I contribute to structural analysis and infrastructure projects.

My interests lie at the intersection of structural engineering, computational mechanics, finite element analysis, scientific computing, and engineering automation.

I received my Master of Science in Structural Engineering from the University of Southern California.
